Our Story

A Hidden Gem
in the Heart of George

Tucked away on Wellington Street in Bodorp, Asian Supermarket George is where the Garden Route goes to find something extraordinary. Whether you're a seasoned traveller missing the bold flavours of Southeast Asia or a curious first-timer, this is your destination.

From hand-made sushi to authentic imported pantry staples, every corner of our store is stocked with quality and passion. It's not just a supermarket — it's a taste journey.
